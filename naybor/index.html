About Naybor Travel Express

"Delivering on neighborly and friendly travels to Nigerians."

OUR MISSION

Naybor Travel Express is a community-driven intercity travel platform built for Nigerians, by Nigerians. We believe that your neighbor's car ride can be your safest, most affordable, and most friendly way to travel across states — from Lagos to Abuja, from Kano to Port Harcourt, and everywhere in between.

We connect verified drivers — everyday people who use their personal vehicles to offer travel services — with passengers seeking reliable intercity transport. Every driver on our platform is manually reviewed and approved by our administration team, ensuring safety and trust.

🛡️

Safety First

Every driver is verified with a valid license and vehicle photos before they can offer any trips on the platform.

🤝

Community Trust

We believe in neighborly trust. Drivers and passengers connect through a transparent, admin-monitored platform.

🗺️

All 36 States

Whether you're travelling from Lagos to Enugu or Sokoto to Kaduna, our network spans the entire Nigerian territory.

💬

Direct Communication

Passengers can chat directly with drivers through our secure in-app messaging system before committing to a trip.

⚡

Fast & Reliable

Real-time trip listings updated constantly. Find available rides for your route in seconds.

💳

Secure Payments

Powered by Paystack — Nigeria's most trusted payment processor for any platform fees.
